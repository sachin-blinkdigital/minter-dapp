ABOUT US

Crypto Runners

is a collection of 10,000 unique NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain, that are created by a design house based in India specialising in digital art and carpet manufacturing, working with skilled artisans and weavers to create beautiful bespoke carpets.

OUR MISSION

Begin Artisans' Charity

for India's skilled weavers. The weavers' magnificent craftsmanship often goes unnoticed and is being greatly replaced by machinery. This makes it difficult for them to make a living as more people lose their jobs and age-old traditions fade away. As a result, we want to assist them as best we can.

ROADMAP

Route to Benevolence

01

Launch

Release the NFTs, of which 5 will be airdropped randomly to early supporters. This is to show appreciation to the OGs that have supported us from day 1, so join our Discord community now!

02

Appreciation

Giveaway exclusive merchandise to holders, starting with T-shirts, hats, and hoodies. You can even send us your minted Crypto Runner to be custom printed.

03

Charity

Begin the Artisans' Charity. Fifty percent of the funds will be allocated towards the charity, and twenty percent will go towards marketing and spreading the word to generate more donations. Thirty percent will be used to facilitate the building and creation of further initiatives, potentially a Season 2 collection.

04

Metaverse

Collaborate with a metaverse game developer to create a game centred on the Crypto Runners. A developer who owns a Crypto Runner will have a better chance of working on this project. Other holders will get exclusive access to the metaverse.

05

Marathon

Organize marathons around the world to introduce Crypto Runners to the real world. Holders get discounted entry fees. Fifty percent of the profits will go to the Artisans' Charity. Forty percent of the profit generated will be used to develop an e-com store where artisans across India can sell their products with bare minimum costs and ten percent towards the founders of the project.
